About

Begin your sweet journey by selecting the perfect cup to house your delectable treat. Whether you prefer a classic ceramic cup or a whimsical, vibrant option, the choice is yours. Once your cup is selected, move on to the next step and explore our array of mouthwatering desserts, each crafted to satisfy your sweet cravings. From velvety cakes to creamy puddings, our menu is a haven for dessert enthusiasts. Don't stop there—make your dessert uniquely yours by choosing from a charming assortment of toppings, adding that extra touch of sweetness. Our cute pink aesthetic adds a sprinkle of charm to your entire experience. After customizing your dream dessert, effortlessly add it to your cart. Transition to the order page, where you can review your selections and proceed to checkout. Complete the experience by filling in your information, and voilà! Your scrumptious creation will soon be on its way to sweeten your day. Indulge in the magic of desserts with a touch of adorable pink perfection!

History

The Sweet Spot was developed by two aspiring second year Students at Vanier College in the Computer Science Program. Sereen Saaida and Valeriya Gergelyuk have collectively came up with the idea to mix their own personal aesthetic preferences and their love for dessert into a personalized website of their page.
